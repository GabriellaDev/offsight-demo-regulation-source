Offshore Wind – Operational and Safety Guidance

Publisher: Demo Authority (based on GOV.UK guidance)
Last updated: 2025-12-10
Document reference: OW-DEMO-001

Operational Reporting

Operators can maintain records of offshore operational checks and make them available to relevant authorities upon request.

A periodic review of safety procedures must be documented, including identified risks and mitigation actions.

Data Sharing

Operators should cooperate with grid stakeholders to support safe and stable offshore wind operations.
